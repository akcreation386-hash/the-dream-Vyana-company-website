Package Description

Venture into the heart of India's royal, historical, and spiritual heritage with our **Golden Triangle Tour with Pushkar**. This carefully designed 7-day circuit effortlessly elevates the standard dynamic trio of Delhi, Agra, and Jaipur by introducing you to the ancient and peaceful spiritual oasis of Pushkar.

Perfect for families, photographers, and history lovers, this loop guides you from the bustling street bazaars and Mughal architectures of Delhi to the unmatched symmetry of the Taj Mahal in Agra, onward to Jaipur's pink fortresses, and finally settles along the peaceful, sacred ghats of Pushkar Lake—home to the world's rare Lord Brahma Temple.

Day-Wise Detailed Itinerary

D1

Arrival in Delhi & Luxury Check-in

Touchdown in the historic capital of India! Our representative will welcome you at Delhi Airport or Railway Station and handle your seamless transfer to your luxury hotel. Spend your day unwinding or enjoy an evening walk around Connaught Place's rich colonial architecture. Overnight stay in Delhi.

D2

Full Day Sightseeing of Old & New Delhi

Immerse yourself in Delhi's rich historical timeline. Walk through the legacy of Mughal rule at the Red Fort, Jama Masjid, and take a rickshaw ride through Chandni Chowk. Later, transition into the structured layout of New Delhi by visiting India Gate, Humayun’s Tomb, the towering Qutub Minar, and driving past the Parliament House. Overnight stay in Delhi.

D3

Drive to Agra & Sunset at Taj Mahal

Check out and take a smooth expressway drive to the Mughal stronghold of Agra. Upon check-in, freshen up and head straight to witness the absolute marvel of the world—the Taj Mahal. Spend your evening watching the white marble glow elegantly under the setting sun. Follow this with an exploration of the massive Agra Fort. Overnight stay in Agra.

D4

Fatehpur Sikri Excursion & Drive to Jaipur

Leave Agra after breakfast. En route to the Pink City of Jaipur, halt at the beautifully preserved sandstone ghost city of Fatehpur Sikri, exploring the massive Buland Darwaza and Panch Mahal. Continue your drive to Jaipur and check into your authentic heritage hotel. Overnight stay in Jaipur.

D5

Exploring Jaipur's Royal Palaces & Forts

Begin your day with a royal jeep/elephant ride up to the grand Amber Fort. In the afternoon, photograph the stunning multi-windowed facade of Hawa Mahal (Palace of Winds). Explore the City Palace museum complex and look at the astronomical precision of Jantar Mantar. Spend your evening shopping for handcrafted gems and block-printed textiles. Overnight stay in Jaipur.

D6

Drive to Sacred Pushkar & Evening Lake Aarti

Drive towards the ancient, sacred town of Pushkar, wrapped beautifully around a sacred lake. Upon arrival, check into your resort and later visit the legendary Brahma Temple, one of the incredibly rare temples in the world dedicated to Lord Brahma. In the evening, walk along the sacred Pushkar Lake Ghats to experience a deeply moving traditional evening prayer (Aarti). Overnight stay in Pushkar.

D7

Pushkar Rose Gardens & Return Drive to Delhi

After breakfast, take a peaceful morning stroll through Pushkar's vast, aromatic rose gardens—which export exotic fragrances worldwide. Check out from your hotel and embark on your scenic return drive to Delhi. Our professional driver will ensure a timely drop-off at Delhi Airport or the Railway Station for your journey home.
