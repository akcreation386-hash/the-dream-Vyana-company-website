Package Description

This Goa Family Holiday Package is a well-rounded 4-day escape built for families, couples and friends who want a relaxed mix of sun, sea and heritage without the hassle of planning. Over three nights you'll move between the buzzing beaches and shacks of North Goa and the calmer, greener shores of South Goa, with an evening river cruise thrown in for good measure.

From lively markets and old Portuguese-era churches to postcard-worthy beaches like Baga, Calangute and Colva, this itinerary is designed to give first-time visitors and repeat travellers alike a well-balanced taste of everything Goa is loved for.

Day-Wise Detailed Itinerary

D1

Arrival in Goa

Land at Goa airport and transfer to your pre-booked hotel or resort. In the evening, enjoy a relaxing boat cruise along the Mandovi River as the sun sets over the water. Overnight stay in Goa.

D2

North Goa Exploration

After breakfast, set out to explore the beaches and buzz of North Goa. Visit Baga Beach, Calangute Beach, Miramar and Dona Paula, and take a walk through Old Goa's historic churches and the ramparts of Fort Aguada. Overnight stay in Goa.

D3

South Goa Discovery

Following breakfast, head south to the quieter beaches of South Goa. Visit Colva Beach along with the Shanta Durga and Mangeshi temples, taking in a calmer, greener side of the state. Overnight stay in Goa.

D4

Departure from Goa

Enjoy a relaxed final breakfast before checking out of the hotel. Our representative will assist with a smooth transfer to the Goa airport or railway station for your onward journey home.
