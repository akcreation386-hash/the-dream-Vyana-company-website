Package Description

Experience India's ultimate cultural circuit with our exclusive Golden Triangle Tour with Dunes. This package brilliantly captures the architectural grandeur of Old & New Delhi, the legendary romance of the Taj Mahal in Agra, and the pink-hued fortresses of Jaipur.

What makes this itinerary completely magical is the addition of the mystical desert dunes. Travel beyond typical monument hopping to indulge in traditional camel safaris, starlit cultural performances, and an authentic taste of desert hospitality across the sun-warm golden sands of Rajasthan.

Day-Wise Detailed Itinerary

D1

Arrival in Delhi & Local Exploration

Welcome to the historic capital of India! Upon arrival at Delhi Airport/Railway Station, receive a warm traditional welcome and get a transfer to your premium hotel. Depending on your arrival time, explore local markets like Connaught Place or Chandni Chowk, experiencing the cultural contrast of Old and New Delhi. Overnight stay in Delhi.

D2

Delhi City Tour & Drive to Agra

After breakfast, proceed for a guided sightseeing tour of Delhi. Marvel at iconic structures including the majestic Red Fort, Qutub Minar, India Gate, and the Lotus Temple. In the afternoon, embark on a comfortable smooth drive to Agra via the Yamuna Expressway. Check into your hotel upon arrival. Overnight stay in Agra.

D3

Taj Mahal Sunrise Tour & Drive to Jaipur

Witness the ethereal beauty of the magnificent Taj Mahal at sunrise, catching the changing hues of white marble under the early sun rays. Return to your hotel for breakfast, checkout, and visit the colossal Agra Fort. Later, drive towards the Pink City of Jaipur, stopping en route at the abandoned Mughal city of Fatehpur Sikri. Check into your Jaipur resort. Overnight stay in Jaipur.

D4

Jaipur Heritage Sightseeing

Dedicate your day to exploring Jaipur's royal legacy. Take an elephant or jeep ride up to the towering gates of the Amber Fort. Later, visit the spectacular Hawa Mahal (Palace of Winds), the City Palace museum complex, and the precise astronomical instruments at Jantar Mantar observatory. In the evening, shop for authentic royal handicrafts, textiles, and gems at Johari Bazar. Overnight stay in Jaipur.

D5

Excursion to the Sand Dunes & Camel Safari

Escape the city crowd today as we take you into the raw landscape of the golden sand dunes of Rajasthan. Arrive at the luxury desert camp and enjoy an unforgettable evening camel safari over rolling ripples of sand. Witness a glorious desert sunset followed by traditional folk music, Kalbeliya dance performances, and a lavish Rajasthani cultural dinner under a star-filled sky. Overnight stay at the desert camp/resort.

D6

Drive Back to Delhi & Departure

Soak in a serene desert sunrise and relish an authentic traditional breakfast. Checkout from the camp and begin your return journey back to Delhi. Our professional driver will ensure a comfortable drop-off at the Delhi Airport or Railway Station for your onward journey, marking the sweet conclusion of your royal holiday circuit.
