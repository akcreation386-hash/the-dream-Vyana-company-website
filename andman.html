Package Description

Discover the enchanting beauty of the Andaman Islands! This extensively curated 6 Nights / 7 Days family package allows you to explore pristine white sandy beaches, swim in crystal-clear turquoise waters, and witness the vibrant marine life that will leave you spellbound.

From rich historical insights at Port Blair's heritage sites to high-speed catamaran cruises linking the hidden beaches of Neil Island, the global wonders of Havelock's Radhanagar Beach, and the exotic underwater ecosystems of Jolly Buoy or Red Skin Island—this loop ensures a comprehensive tropical experience.

Day-Wise Detailed Itinerary

D1

Arrival in Port Blair & Viper Island Excursion

Welcome to Port Blair! Upon arrival, receive a smooth transfer to your pre-booked hotel. In the afternoon, embark on an excursion to the historical Viper Island, uncovering early heritage. In the evening, enjoy a brilliant sunset view at Corbyn's Cove Beach. Overnight stay in Port Blair.

D2

High-Speed Catamaran to Neil Island

After an early breakfast, hop onto a high-speed catamaran cruise heading straight to Neil Island. The afternoon is free to explore the tropical property. In the evening, head out to Neil Island's pristine coastlines and visit Laxmanpur Beach to experience one of the most magnificent beach sunsets in India. Overnight stay in Neil Island.

D3

Neil Island Exploration & Ferry to Havelock

After breakfast, visit the iconic Sitapur and Bharatpur Beaches on Neil Island, famous for white sands and optional beach sports. In the afternoon, board the cruise towards Havelock Island. Check into your beach resort and spend a relaxing evening. Overnight stay in Havelock Island.

D4

Elephant Beach Cruise & Snorkeling

Proceed to Elephant Beach after breakfast, renowned globally for thrilling water adventures. This package includes an immersive snorkeling excursion in the warm, ultra-transparent, liquid-blue waters off Elephant Beach to view spectacular shallow reefs. Overnight stay in Havelock Island.

D5 -->

Radhanagar Beach & Return to Port Blair

Spend your morning relaxing or beach hopping around the world-famous Radhanagar Beach. Its emerald waters and powdery shores are perfect for family photographs. In the afternoon, catch the return catamaran back to Port Blair. Check-in and relax. Overnight stay in Port Blair.

D6

Jolly Buoy / Red Skin Island Expedition

Early morning, head to the Phoenix Bay Jetty to catch a ferry towards the Mahatma Gandhi Marine National Park ecosystem at Jolly Buoy or Red Skin Island (approx. 29 km away). Dive deep into pristine coral fields via snorkeling, glass-bottom boat rides, or scuba diving (optional). Return to Port Blair for your final evening. Overnight stay in Port Blair.

D7

Port Blair City Tour & Departure

After breakfast, explore Port Blair's urban heritage by visiting the Anthropological Museum, Cellular Jail, and the local handicraft emporiums. Afterwards, get transferred to the airport for your flight home with beautiful tropical memories.
