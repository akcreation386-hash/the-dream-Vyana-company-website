Package Description

Welcome to the definitive royal odyssey. The **Grand Rajasthan Travel Plan** is a meticulously orchestrated 13-day itinerary crafted to take you deep into the heart of India's most celebrated heritage corridor. This is not just a holiday; it's a passage through time that unveils the ultimate imperial loop of palaces, historic fortresses, and rustic desert landscapes.

Designed flawlessly for heritage connoisseurs, families, and global explorers, this journey seamlessly blends the historical monuments of Delhi and Agra with Rajasthan’s vast topographical marvels. Experience the open-air frescoes of Shekhawati, the golden desert fortifications of Jaisalmer, the blue-washed alleys of Jodhpur, and the sparkling romantic lakes of Udaipur, before concluding along the sacred ghats of Pushkar.

Day-Wise Detailed Itinerary

D1

Arrival in Delhi & Luxury Orientation

Arrive in India's capital city, Delhi. Meet our representative at the airport/railway station and transfer to your premium hotel. Spend the rest of the day relaxing or exploring the high-end shopping and dining avenues of New Delhi at your own pace. Overnight stay in Delhi.

D2

Delhi Historic City Tour & Drive to Agra

Embark on a captivating guided exploration of Old and New Delhi. Discover monument highlights like the Red Fort, Jama Masjid, Qutub Minar, and India Gate. In the afternoon, check out and drive via the Yamuna Expressway to the Mughal capital of Agra. Check-in and relax. Overnight stay in Agra.

D3

Taj Mahal at Sunrise & Drive to Jaipur via Fatehpur Sikri

Witness the ethereal beauty of the Taj Mahal at sunrise as the white marble catches the golden morning glow. Explore the majestic Agra Fort before setting off towards the Pink City of Jaipur. En route, tour the red sandstone ruins of the ghost city of Fatehpur Sikri. Arrive in Jaipur and check into your heritage property. Overnight stay in Jaipur.

D4

Full Day Exploration of Royal Jaipur

Enjoy a royal morning jeep or elephant ride up to the Amber Fort gates. Later, capture photographs at the multi-windowed Hawa Mahal (Palace of Winds), tour the royal collections inside the City Palace, and marvel at the massive stone astronomical instruments at Jantar Mantar. End your day shopping for gems and textiles at local bazaars. Overnight stay in Jaipur.

D5

Drive to Mandawa (Shekhawati Open-Air Gallery)

Check out and drive to the historic semi-desert Shekhawati region, stopping at Mandawa. Famous as Rajasthan's "open-air art gallery," explore the stunning 18th and 19th-century havelis adorned with vibrant hand-painted frescoes and intricate mirror work. Overnight stay in a traditional Mandawa heritage haveli.

D6

Drive to Bikaner via Junagarh Fort

Travel deeper into the semi-arid landscape to Bikaner, an ancient oasis town on the old caravan trade routes. Visit the unique, un-conquered Junagarh Fort, renowned for its architectural blend of Rajput and Mughal styles, and explore the ancient red-sandstone havelis of the old city. Overnight stay in Bikaner.

D7

Drive to Jaisalmer (The Golden Desert City)

Set out across the heart of the Thar Desert toward Jaisalmer, known globally as the Golden City due to the yellow sandstone used in its architecture. Catch your first glimpse of the living fort rising like a sandcastle from the desert floor as you arrive. Check into your hotel and enjoy a peaceful evening. Overnight stay in Jaisalmer.

D8

Jaisalmer Fort Exploration & Luxury Desert Camp Safari

Tour the majestic Jaisalmer Fort (Sonargarh), exploring its narrow, lively lanes, ancient Jain temples, and carved havelis like Patwon-ki-Haveli. In the afternoon, travel to the Sam Sand Dunes. Experience an exotic sunset camel safari, followed by traditional Rajasthani folk music, Kalbeliya dance performances, and a royal dinner under the stars. Overnight stay in luxury desert tents.

D9

Drive to Jodhpur (The Blue City)

Bid farewell to the desert dunes and drive eastward to Jodhpur, the Blue City. Check into your hotel and head out to climb the colossal Mehrangarh Fort, which stands on a cliff 400 feet above the city, offering panoramic views of blue-washed houses. Visit the pristine white marble Jaswant Thada cenotaph. Overnight stay in Jodhpur.

D10

Drive to Udaipur via Ranakpur Jain Temples

Travel south toward Udaipur, the City of Lakes. En route, stop in the quiet Aravali valleys to wonder at the Ranakpur Jain Temples, internationally acclaimed for their 1,444 intricately carved unique marble pillars. Continue your drive to Udaipur and check into your lake-facing property. Overnight stay in Udaipur.

D11

Full Day Sightseeing of Romantic Udaipur

Spend a dreamy day discovering Udaipur's palaces. Tour the massive City Palace complex along Lake Pichola, visit Jagdish Temple, and stroll through the fountains and marble pavilions of Saheliyon-ki-Bari (Garden of the Maids). In the evening, enjoy a scenic boat cruise on Lake Pichola past the Lake Palace. Overnight stay in Udaipur.

D12

Drive to Holy Pushkar via Ajmer Sharif

Drive northward to the sacred town of Pushkar. Pause en route in Ajmer to pay respects at the holy Dargah Sharif of Khwaja Moinuddin Chishti. Arrive in Pushkar, visit the unique Lord Brahma Temple, and witness the evening prayer rituals and oil lamp reflections along the holy Pushkar Lake Ghats. Overnight stay in Pushkar.

D13

Return Drive to Delhi & Departure

After an early breakfast, take a morning stroll through the vibrant local rose markets of Pushkar. Check out and proceed with a comfortable return drive back to Delhi. Our professional driver will ensure a direct drop-off at Delhi Airport or the Railway Station for your onward journey home.
